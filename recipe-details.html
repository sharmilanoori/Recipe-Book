Cheese & Herb Stuffed Eggs

Ingredients

  • Hard-boiledeggs
  • White cheese:50-70 gr
  • Fresh herbs(dill, parsley): 1 tablespoon
  • Mayonnaise or yogurt: 1 tablespoon
  • Salt and pepper:to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil the eggs and cut them in half.
  2. Remove the yolks and mash them.
  3. Mix the yolks with cheese, herbs, mayo or yogurt, sait and pepper.
  4. Fill the egg with this mixture
  5. Optinal: garnish with paprika or extra herbs
